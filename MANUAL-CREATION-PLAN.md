## 1. Introduction
- Overview of Kitchen One App

### Overview of Kitchen One App
Kitchen One is a comprehensive restaurant management and food ordering platform designed to streamline operations for both customers and restaurant staff. The app provides a seamless experience for ordering food, managing deliveries, and handling restaurant operations, all through an intuitive interface.
### Key Features and Benefits
#### For Customers:
- **Convenient Ordering**: Browse menu, customize orders, and place them with just a few taps
- **Real-time Tracking**: Track your order from preparation to delivery
- **Multiple Payment Options**: Secure payment methods including credit/debit cards and digital wallets
- **Order History**: Easy access to past orders and favorites
- **Personalized Experience**: Tailored recommendations based on order history
#### For Restaurant Staff:
- **Efficient Order Management**: Real-time order updates and notifications
- **Inventory Control**: Track ingredients and manage stock levels
- **Customer Management**: Maintain customer profiles and order history
- **Analytics Dashboard**: Monitor sales, popular items, and business performance
#### For Delivery Personnel:
- **Order Assignment**: Receive and manage delivery assignments
- **Earnings Tracking**: Monitor deliveries and earnings in real-time
### System Requirements
#### Mobile App Requirements:
- **iOS**: iOS 14.0 or later
  - Compatible with iPhone 6s and newer
  - Requires approximately 50MB of free space
- **Android**: Android 8.0 (Oreo) or later
  - Minimum 2GB RAM
  - Requires approximately 50MB of free space
#### Web Browser Requirements:
- Latest version of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge
- JavaScript and cookies must be enabled
- Minimum screen resolution: 320x480 pixels
#### Internet Connection:
- Minimum 3G/4G/LTE or stable Wi-Fi connection
- At least 2 Mbps download speed for optimal performance
